Ingredients

  • 1/2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 miso paste or soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth/bouillon
  • 1 packaged instant ramen
  • 2 medium sized bok choy
  • 1 slice of pork belly (optional - can be substituted for crab cake or chicken or tofu)
  • 1 large egg (optional)
  • 1 green onion sliced
  • 1 handful of bean sprouts or spinach
  • sriracha for taste
  • 1 small norri sheet for garnish
